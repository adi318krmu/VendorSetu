About VendorSetu

VendorSetu is a marketplace platform connecting food vendors and suppliers. Vendors can browse, compare, and order quality food products from trusted suppliers, while suppliers can showcase their products and manage orders efficiently. The platform supports FSSAI certification for suppliers, ensuring food safety and compliance. Built with a modern tech stack, VendorSetu aims to streamline the food supply chain for restaurants, cafes, and food businesses across India.

  • Find and connect with verified suppliers
  • Order a wide range of food products
  • Supplier FSSAI certification for food safety
  • Easy-to-use dashboards for vendors and suppliers
  • Secure and efficient order management
